The Top Anime & Movies According To Kapil

My Top 3 Anime and Movies

Your Name

Your Name is a 2016 Japanese animated romantic fantasy film written and directed by Makoto Shinkai, produced by CoMix Wave Films and distributed by Toho.

One Piece

One Piece (stylized in all caps) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda.

Naruto

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto.
